# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for everyone who contributes to or participates in our projects.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and professional in all interactions
* Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
* Provide and accept constructive feedback gracefully
* Focus on what is best for the community and project

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment, discrimination, or intimidatory behavior of any kind
* Disrespectful, unprofessional, or inappropriate language
* Any conduct that creates an unsafe or unwelcoming environment

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to [opensource@inotiv.com](mailto:opensource@inotiv.com). All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in consequences determined by project maintainers, ranging from a warning to permanent removal from the project.
